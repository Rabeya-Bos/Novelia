Project Name

Novelia – Read Beyond Imagination

Overview

Novelia is a digital reading platform that enables users to discover and read thousands of web novels and ebooks across various genres. The platform provides personalized recommendations, an intuitive reading experience, bookmarking, and seamless access across devices.

Vision

To become a leading online reading platform that connects readers with engaging stories from around the world.

Mission

To make reading accessible, enjoyable, and personalized for everyone.
